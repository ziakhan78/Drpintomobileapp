At Dr.Pinto & Associates we enhance and improve patient care through new advances in dental technologies. Our office uses digital radiography with state-of-the-art equipment and software. This allows us to improve the quality, and diagnostic value of the xray.

Advantages of using digital dental radiographs:
  • Significant lower dose of radiation by 80-90%.
  • Increased patient comfort.
  • Instant image.
  • Enhanced image quality.
  • Image enlargement, and client education tools.
  • Environmentally friendly- no chemical developers.
