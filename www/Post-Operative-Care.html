Extraction Post-Operative Care: Instruction for Home Care

It is important to take care of your surgery site.

Please follow the following instructions.
  • Bite on gauze for 30 minutes to stop any bleeding. If bleeding continues, place another piece of gauze and continue to apply pressure. For at least 24 hours:
    • Do not Smoke
    • Do not drink through a straw
    • Do not drink alcohol, and carbonated beverages (pop)
    • Do not drink hot liquids
    • Do not blow your nose
    • Dot not spit excessively
  • Do maintain a soft diet for the remainder of the day. Warm soups, ice cream and milk shakes are all great examples. Remember - NO straws.
  • If your pain, discomfort or bleeding continues or worsens, call the office for a re-examination.
  • If you were given medications, take them according to the instructions. If you have pain medication at home, you may take them according to the instructions.
  • Tomorrow morning, start rinsing gently with warm salt water, every 4 hours for 2 days. (1/4 teaspoon of salt to 1 cup of water)
  • If any problems or questions arise, please feel free to call the office on (905) 615-1078 or your dentist at home (416) 779-8769.
