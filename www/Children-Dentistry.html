At Dr. Pinto & Associates, we accept and treat children of all ages. Our goal is to make every visit a positive experience, in order to create a lifetime of good oral health and habits for your child. Our office is a child-friendly environment. We have a play area in the waiting room, which includes a lego station, a video game station, and a television. All operatories have a television so your child can watch their favourite channel during their dental appointment.

Your child should first visit the dentist by the age of two. The first dental appointment will be for a "fun visit". At this appointment, our friendly team will introduce your child to the dental environment. This includes, giving your child a ride in the dental chair, and showing them all the dental equipment. We will also speak to you and your child about the importance of visiting the dentist, practicing good oral hygiene, dietary advise, and answer all your questions. We strongly believe in making the first visit stress-free, which will allow us to build a trusting relationship with your child. After the fun visit, we will arrange another day to have your child return for a new patient exam and a dental cleaning.
