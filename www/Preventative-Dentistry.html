Preventative DentistryBack






Routine dental hygiene visits are essential for maintaining healthy teeth, gums, and contribute to the overall wellness of your health. Our dental hygiene team are experienced professionals, who are dedicated in helping our patients achieve optimal oral health. At a dental hygiene appointment, plaque, tartar, and stain are removed to help prevent gum disease and tooth decay. Through regular periodontal screenings, we are able to detect early signs of gum disease, and treat these areas prior to the progression and breakdown of the surrounding tissues.

A routine dental check-up, and x-rays are also done as required. This allows for early detection of periodontal disease & tooth decay. Our goal at Dr. Pinto & Associates, is to ensure that every dental hygiene appointment is “pain free”. We offer many different pain reducing treatment options such as topical gels, local anesthetics, and sedation to ensure patient comfort. Oral hygiene instructions and dietary recommendations are also reviewed at every dental hygiene appointment. We are dedicated to work “together” with you to achieve your oral health goals.
