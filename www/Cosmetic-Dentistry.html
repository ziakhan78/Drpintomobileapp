Advances in Cosmetic Dentistry make it possible for dental professionals to help patients achieve spectacular esthetics. At Dr Pinto & Associates we offer a wide range of Cosmetic Dentistry services: Tooth whitening, Veneers, Crowns, Invisalign, Implants and beautiful replacement of missing teeth both fixed and removable.

Whether you're looking to fix a single crooked tooth or desire more extensive cosmetic work, the dedicated team at Dr Pinto & Associates is at your service to provide gentle and cost-effective cosmetic dental treatments. When you visit our practice, you will benefit from our individualized, attentive approach to cosmetic dentistry. And because we want you to be comfortable with your course of treatment, we'll take the time to address your concerns before we get started.

Dr. Pinto and Dr. Renaldo are both members of The American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (AACD)
