Q. Is your dental office accepting new clients?

A. Yes. At Dr. Pinto & Associates we are always accepting new patients, and referrals.

Q. What can I expect to be done at my first dental appointment?

It is recommended that all new patients have a complete oral examination, along with xrays. This includes a thorough extra-oral and intra-oral exam, an oral cancer screening, gum assessment, and a full set of xrays. This baseline data will allow us to understand the current status of your oral health, and will assist us in creating an individualized treatment plan that best fits your needs.

Q. At what age should I bring my child in for their first dental visit?

It is recommended that your child first visit the dentist by the age of 2.

Q. Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes. As a courtesy to our patients, our dental office accepts assignment of benefits. This allows the dental plan provider to pay the benefits covered directly to our dental office. However, some dental plan providers pay the benefits covered directly to the patient. In this case, the patient is responsible for all costs at each dental visit.

http://www.oda.on.ca/you-your-dentist/dental-benefits-explained91

Q. Does your office do tooth colored restorations?

Yes. Tooth colored composite resins is the primary restorative material we use when filling a tooth. This material comes in many different shades, which allows us to precisely match the restoration to the natural color of your tooth.

Q. Do you place dental implants?

Yes. At Dr. Pinto & Associates, dental implants are one of the treatment option for replacing a missing tooth. We can also place implants to improve the fit and comfort of your existing dentures. Free missing tooth consultations are available for all clients.

Q. Can I straighten my teeth without wearing braces?

Yes. At Dr. Pinto & Associates, we offer Invisalign as an alternative to wearing fixed visible braces. Invisalign is a popular orthodontic treatment option which consists of wearing removable clear alignments (trays). Our dental office offers free consultations, to assess if invisalign is the best treatment option for you.

http://www.invisalign.com

Q. Does your office do wisdom teeth extractions?

Yes. For the convenience of our patients, we have a dental surgeon who specializes in wisdom teeth extractions under sedation (asleep dentistry).

Q. Can I have gum surgery at your dental office?

Yes. Dr. David Chvartszaid is a highly trained and experienced gum specialist, who works at our dental office. He specialized in a wide range of different gum surgery treatments. Gum grafting, surgery for gum disease, and bone grafting.

Q. How can you help me cope with my dental anxiety?

At Dr. Pinto & Associates we understand the impact that dental anxiety, fear, or phobia may have on your willingness to have dental treatment that you need. We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral sedation (tablets), and deep sedation (I.V).
