×Dr. Renaldo Pinto

BDS, MDS, DDS
Our Director

Dr. Renaldo Pinto obtained his Bachelor of Dental Surgery from Nair Hospital Dental College (University of Mumbai). Upon completion of dental school in 1985, he obtained his Masters in Pediatric dentistry in 1988. Dr. Renaldo Pinto completed the two year program at the University of Toronto, and obtained his DDS in 2005. He was inducted the Omicron Kappa Upsilon Dental Honors Society, along with the Robert Pownall Award for Academic Achievement.

Dr. Renaldo Pinto is devoted to his practice, and creating and maintaing positive experiences and relationships with his patients. His gentle approach and in-depth expertise are a combination his patients appreciate. With over 30 years of dental experience, Dr. Renaldo Pinto offers expertise in many areas of dentistry, in particular dental implants.

When not in the office, you can find Dr. Renaldo enjoying life’s simple pleasures; spending time with family and friends, reading, boating, and walking his three greyhound dogs.

×Dr. Aparna Pinto

BDS, DDS
Our Director

Dr. Aparna Pinto completed her Doctorate of Dental Surgery in 1986 at Nair Hospital Dental College (University of Mumbai). Dr. Aparna Pinto completed the two year program at the University of Toronto, and obtained her DDS in 2006. She was inducted the Omicron Kappa Upsilon Dental Honors Society.

Dr. Aparna Pinto enjoys all aspects of general dentistry, with a keen interest in dental prosthetics. She also works at the University of Toronto, where she is able to share her interests and expertise of dental prosthetics with students. Dr. Aparna Pinto has a true passion for dentistry, which is clearly shown through her work, and relationships with her patients. Her goal is to ensure that she fulfills patient’s goals and needs, and allows every patient to leave the office smiling.

When not in the office, Dr. Aparna Pinto enjoys cooking, boating, spending time with family and friends, and walking her three greyhound dogs.
